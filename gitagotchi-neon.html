gitagotchi · neon sign (animated night-neon)

The night-neon colorway brought to life like a real neon sign: the tubes buzz on letter by letter, settle into a slow breathing glow, and every so often a tube flickers the way a tired sign does. White-hot cores, blue→purple halo, and a soft reflection on the floor.

glow buzz state powering on…

How it's animated: same 7-tall pixel typeface as the wordmark, but each letter tracks its own brightness. On power-on they ignite in sequence with a couple of false-start buzzes; then a global sine breathing drives the halo blur, and a Poisson-ish random picks a letter now and then for a 60–160 ms flicker. Each lit pixel is drawn in three passes — a wide blurred halo, a tighter tube, and a near-white core — which is what sells "neon" rather than "colored text". All procedural, so it re-skins to any two-color glow.
