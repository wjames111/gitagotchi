gitagotchi · holding a spear — the guardian pose

The pet stands guard with a spear. Fully procedural: a shaft planted beside the body, a spearhead + binding above it, a paw gripping mid-shaft, and a determined brow. Works on all 50 species; the spear can be planted on either side (--flip), and there's a small guard-tap idle where the butt of the spear knocks the ground.

gh pet — spear

the guardian pose

The spear is a full-height prop: a 1px shaft from the ground to above the head, a triangular steel head and a red binding near the tip. A gripping paw (body-colored, dark-outlined) reaches out to hold the shaft at mid-body, and the eye row gains a determined \ / brow. On the guard-tap frames the whole spear drops 1px and a dust pixel puffs at its base.
an accessory / cosmetic, not a state — a fun unlockable pose (e.g. earned via a "streak defender" achievement, or just gh pet --pose spear). Held to the pet's outer edge so it never covers the face; stacks with beard, glasses, seasons.

frame strip · guard-tap cycle

every guardian — same spear, fifty grips

Anchor logic: the same body-geometry finder used by the book — locate the eye row and the body's vertical extent, plant the shaft just outside the widest body column on the chosen side, and place the grip pixel where the arm would reach (mid-body, one column in from the shaft). Because it keys off measured geometry, a tall penguin grips high and a low blob grips low, all automatically. Nothing new in the sprite files.
