Design decisions: bars are colored by owner, not by value — in a
  comparison, identity is the channel that matters, and the printed number carries magnitude.
  The ◂/▸ marker (green, with the value bolded) is the only "score". Curiosity and wisdom sit
  below the dashed rule as the slow stats; health is structurally absent rather than shown
  empty. s prints this exact frame as plain text (gh pet
  compare mona --snapshot) for pasting into a README or a group chat — the shareable
  moment is the whole point of this screen.
